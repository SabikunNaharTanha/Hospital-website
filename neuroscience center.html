PeopleCare Neuroscience Centre


Country's First One Stop Comprehensive Neuro Solution in a Private Hospital

Square Neuroscience Centre is a centre of excellence, providing high quality personalized care through an eminent faculty of Neuro-medicine, Neuro-surgery, Neuro-anesthesia, Psychiatry, Radiology and Imaging along with Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation.

In house EEG, EMG, NCV (Nerve conduction velocity) tests and state of art facilities for DSE and coiling procedures are available.

OPD services Square Neuroscience Centre has an absolutely new dedicated spacious OPD area with fully-equipped medical &
surgical consultation & procedure rooms for all kinds of Neuro-medicine and Neurosurgical procedures.
IPD services Large, comfortable suites, single & twin-sharing rooms with full time round the clock doctors and nursing support including dietitian supervised meals.
AMBULANCE Fully equipped critical care ambulance service with special resuscitation trained staff. We also have our own Air Ambulance service to transport patients from any corner of the country.
EMERGENCY ER is equipped with all lifesaving support for critically ill patients including ventilators, defibrillators and monitors. Emergency Room (ER) provides non-stop life-saving care 24 hours a day.
NEURO ICU Neuro ICU with step down arrangements is a cutting edge Neuro-intensive care unit, with all modern equipment & facilities run by full time consultant, post graduate specialist doctors and neuro-trained nurses serving round the clock.
NEURO OT State of the art Neuro OT is managed by full time Neuro anesthesiologists and critical care specialists. There are trained nurses and technicians well versed with the ventilators and monitors for close neurological monitoring.
