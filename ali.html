DR.Qashim Ali

Specialty - Senior Consultant, microboilogy Degree - MBBS, DA, FCPS

DR.Qashim Ali graduated from Sir Salimullah Medical College & Mitford Hospital in 1982. After his internship, he worked as medical officer in The Islamic Republic of Iran for four years. After coming back in 1988, he joined in Holy Family Red Crescent Medical College Hospital in the department of Anesthesiology. He obtained advanced specialized training from the Institute of Post Graduate Medicine & Research (IPGMR) and completed his Diploma in Anesthesiology in 1991 and FCPS in 1993.

DR.Qashim Ali became a Consultant in the year 1994 in Holy Family Red Crescent Medical College Hospital and later he worked as Senior Consultant & Associate Professor of Anesthesiology & the Head of the Department of Intensive Care Medicine from the year 2001. He was also an examiner of BCPS and Instructor in Nepal and Philippines for a USAID sponsored course on hospital management, emergencies and disaster management. He is working Square Hospital as Senior Consultant, Anesthesiology.
