Peoplecare Child Development Centre (CDC)



Our motto is to prevent disability, optimize development and improve quality of survival of all children in Bangladesh. To establish child and family-friendly Child Developmental Centre (CDC) we apply strategies and tools standardized for Bangladeshi children for early screening, assessment, intervention, treatment and management of the entire range of neurodevelopmental disorders and disabilities likeo

  1. Cerebral Palsy
  2. Autism spectrum disorders
  3. Speech and language delays
  4. Visual and hearing impairments
  5. Intellectual developmental disorder
  6. Epilepsy
  7. Hyperactive Child
  8. Psychosocial issues
  9. School refusal, temper tantrum,
    conduct disorder, OCD
  10. Undiagnosed and rare conditions,
    syndromic, congenital malformation

We provide psychosocial services to families and empower parents and primary care-providers to optimize their child's development.

Available services in CDC

  1. Developmental therapy - Poor neck control, sitting and standing problem, stiff limbs
  2. Sitting and feeding - Problem in feeding
  3. Speech therapy - Speech problem
  4. Vision Impairment - Early detection and stimulation therapy
  5. Hyperactive Child - Assessment and management
  6. Intellectual Disability - Assessment and management
  7. Anxiety disorder - Anxious, stressed child
