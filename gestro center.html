Peoplecare Gastroenterology Centre



Each patient is unique. Our team of Gastroenterologists and Hepatologists with national and international training, qualification and experience ensures that you are treated comprehensively, individually, uniquely.

At GI & Hepatology Center we provide almost all types endoscopic services
which include.

  1. Diagnostic Oesophago-gastro-duodenoscopy, Colonoscopy and ERCP
  2. All therapeutic options for Variceal Bleeding
  3. Oesophageal Dilatation and stenting
  4. Polypectomy
  5. Biliary and pancreatic stone extraction, duct dilatation and stenting

This Center ensures:

  1. Probably the best GI service in the country.
  2. Round the clock emergency coverage
  3. Maintain strict infection-control protocol
  4. All endoscopic Procedure is done under sedation unless otherwise indicated.
